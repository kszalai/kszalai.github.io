Featured Work

LEGO NXT Bluetooth Robot Android Controller

This app is a controller for a Lego NXT Mindstorms Robot. Connection is done through Bluetooth. This controller lets you drive your robot with 2 servo motors. My team and I made 2 modes of driving available in the app.

  • Directional Touch Driving
  • Accelerometer Driving
The app will also tell you how much battery is left on your robot. There is also a tab in the app where you can see the sensors available on the robot, but that is not functional as it was not required for the class.

Check out the repository on GitHub!
